    What is data privacy ?

    Data privacy means protecting personal information and ensuring that individuals have control over how their data is collected, used, and shared. It is about keeping sensitive details such as names, addresses, phone numbers, financial information, and online activities safe from misuse or unauthorized access. In today‚Äôs digital world, where people use websites, apps, and social media every day, data privacy has become very important.

  • Protecting personal information (name, phone, email, address, ID numbers, etc.)

  • Controlling access ‚Äì making sure only authorized people/organizations can see or use your data

  • Transparency ‚Äì knowing how, why, and where your data is being used

  • Consent ‚Äì companies must ask permission before collecting or sharing your information

  • Data security ‚Äì keeping information safe from hackers, leaks, or misuse

  • Right to delete or correct data ‚Äì users should have the option to update or remove their information

    • Why is Data Privacy Important?

    1. Protects people from identity theft and fraud

    3. Helps you keep control of your digital life

    4. Many countries have laws (like GDPR in Europe, PDP Bill in India) to protect data privacy
